Speaking & advisory

Bringing 20 years of science and policy experience to conferences, panels, and boards.

Topics I speak on

Keynotes & expert talks

01

Water safety & pathogen monitoring

The science of keeping drinking and recreational water safe — from qPCR detection to early-warning systems and policy implications for urban water security.

02

Non-sewered sanitation & global standards

What ISO 30500 and ISO 31800 mean for sanitation access in the Global South — and how standards, funding, and implementation must align to deliver impact.

03

Sustainability leadership in institutions

How universities, corporates, and government bodies can build genuine sustainability capacity — beyond rankings and reporting, into culture and operations.

04

Circular economy in sanitation & waste

Resource recovery from faecal sludge, food waste, and menstrual waste — the evidence base, the policy frameworks, and the implementation gaps that need closing.

05

Women in science & leadership

Personal and structural perspectives on advancing women in STEM — from laboratory culture to board representation.

06

Science-to-policy translation

How researchers can communicate findings to policymakers, funders, and civil society — and how institutions can build the infrastructure for evidence-based governance.
